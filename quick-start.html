Quick Start Guide

Your complete 7-step walkthrough — from injection to harvest.

What You Need:

  • Your ShroomBloom Plus kit (includes bag, box, wipes, gloves, rubber band)
  • Spore syringe or liquid culture (get yours from our partners with code Bloom15)
  • Clean scissors
  • Spray bottle with clean water
1
Clean & Inject

Turn off fans and close windows — still air is your friend. Put on gloves and wipe them down with the large alcohol wipe. Wipe the entire bag, then the injection port carefully with the small wipe.

Shake your syringe, insert the needle straight into the injection port, and inject 2–3 cc toward the bottom front of the bag. Remove the needle and inflate the bag slightly by holding it by the top seal for 1 minute.

⚡ Tip: The injection port is the most critical spot to clean — this single wipe is what protects you from contamination.
Time: Day 1 | Next: Wait 3–14 days
2
Colonization Phase

Place your bag in the box (filter patch out the top). Store it in a dark, warm spot like a closet or shelf — 72–78°F is ideal. Liquid culture shows white growth in 3–7 days. Spore syringes take 7–14 days. Just wait and watch.

What you're looking for: White, fuzzy, thread-like mycelium spreading through the grain. What to avoid: Don't open the bag. Don't shake it. Just leave it alone.

🌡️ Temperature is key: Keep it between 72–78°F. A consistent 75°F in a closet beats a sunny windowsill that fluctuates.
Time: 3–14 days | Next: Mix & Shake
3
Break & Shake

Once about 20–30% of the grain shows white mycelium, it's time to mix. With the bag still sealed, use your hands to gently break apart the colonized grain and mix it with the rest — like kneading dough.

Flatten it into an even layer. Wrap the rubber band snugly around the bottom to prevent side pinning. Re-inflate the bag, put it back in the box, and wait again.

✓ Why mix? Mixing spreads colonized grain throughout, giving your mycelium dozens of new starting points and cutting colonization time in half.
Time: Day after mixing | Next: Wait 1–2 weeks
4
Full Colonization

Your job is patience. Keep the bag in the dark box at 75°F. Check weekly for white growth spreading through the substrate. You're done when the entire block looks mostly white all the way through.

No misting yet. No opening. The bag is sealed and self-regulating through the 0.2-micron filter patch.

⏱️ Timing: Total from injection to full colonization is usually 2–4 weeks. Liquid culture moves faster than spore syringes.
Time: 1–3 weeks | Next: Hoodie Cut
5
The Hoodie Cut

Block fully white? Time to trigger fruiting. Using clean scissors, cut a U-shape into the bag just above the substrate block: left side, bottom, and right side. Leave the back attached so it folds down like a hoodie.

Fold the flap backward to expose the substrate. Place your box in indirect light or under a desk lamp (12 hours on/off). Fan once a day by lifting the flap and waving it 5–10 times to push out CO2 and bring in fresh oxygen.

💡 Why the hoodie? The flap creates a humidity tent while CO2 escapes. You get most of the humidity and air management for free — the bag does the work.
Time: After cutting | Next: Watch for pins
6
Fruiting & Pins

Within 5–10 days, small bumps called "pins" will form on the substrate surface. These are baby mushrooms. Keep fanning once daily. If the surface looks dry and cracked, mist lightly with clean water. If you see water droplets on the walls, you're perfect.

The pins will grow into full mushrooms over the next 7–10 days.

✓ Moisture & air balance: Too much misting and no air = fuzzy surface but no pins. Too dry = cracked surface. Fan consistently and mist as needed.
Time: 5–10 days for pins | 7–10 days to harvest
7
Harvest & Repeat

Harvest when caps start to flatten and the veil tears away from the stem — don't wait too long. Twist at the base and pull gently. Pick ALL mushrooms at once, even tiny ones, to give the substrate a fresh start.

Want more? Mist generously, clip the flap partially shut, and mist daily. Most ShroomBloom kits produce 2–4 flushes over several weeks.

🎯 Pick before the veil breaks to maximize future flushes. Early harvest = more flushes.
Repeat: Mist daily for 1–2 weeks until next pins form

Golden Rules

Golden Rules

🌡️ Temperature
Keep between 72–78°F. Consistency matters more than perfection.
⏱️ Patience
Don't rush. Patience beats perfection. Rushing leads to contamination.
✓ Cleanliness
One thorough wipe of the injection port prevents 90% of contamination issues.
🎯 Follow Steps
Strong genetics + clean technique = guaranteed success. We replace contaminated kits free.
